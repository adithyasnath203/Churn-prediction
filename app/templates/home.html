A product to find customers who are ready to leave.
For Subscription based companies

This project aims to develop a machine learning model capable of predicting customer churn, which is when a customer stops using a service or making purchases. By analyzing customer data, the model will identify patterns and behaviors associated with customers who are likely to churn. This allows us to proactively intervene with targeted promotional emails or personalized offers. By reaching out to at-risk customers with relevant incentives, we can potentially re-engage them, improve customer retention, and ultimately boost customer lifetime value.
